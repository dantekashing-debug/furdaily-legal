Common Questions

How do I create a pet profile?

Open the app, go to the "Profile" tab, tap "Pet Profile," then tap the "+" button to add your pet. You can add multiple pets and switch between them anytime.

How do I report a lost pet?

Go to the "Nearby" tab, tap the "+" button at the bottom center, and choose "Lost Pet." Fill in your pet's details, last-seen location, and upload photos. Nearby users will be alerted.

How do adoption listings work?

All adoption listings are user-to-user. FurDaily does not handle any transactions, deposits, or funds. Any deposit amount shown is a reference value stated by the posting user — all payment arrangements are strictly private and happen offline, in person, after the pet is delivered.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account and Data" at the bottom. Your account enters a 7-day cooling-off period. If you do not log back in during that time, all your data is permanently removed.

How do I block or report a user?

Tap any user's avatar to open their profile, then tap the "⋯" menu to block or report. You can manage your blocked list in Settings → Privacy → Blocked Users.

Will my location be shared publicly?

No. FurDaily only requests "When In Use" location permission and applies a 500-meter random offset to any coordinates displayed to other users. Your precise location is never shared.

I found a bug or have a feature request

Please email us at dantekashing@gmail.com with a clear description. Screenshots are very helpful. We read every message.

Safety & Fraud Prevention

If you are arranging an adoption or responding to a lost pet reward, please keep yourself safe:

  • Always meet in person in a public place.
  • Only pay any deposit or reward after the pet is physically delivered to you.
  • Verify the pet's identity through unique features (markings, temperament, response to commands).
  • Anyone demanding upfront payment or asking you to wire funds is very likely a scammer — report them immediately.

常见问题

如何创建宠物档案？

打开 App，进入「我的」标签页，点击「宠物档案」，然后点击「+」按钮添加宠物。您可以添加多只宠物并随时切换。

如何发布走失宠物报告？

进入「附近」标签页，点击底部中央的「+」按钮，选择「走失宠物」，填写宠物信息、最后出现位置并上传照片。附近的用户会收到提示。

送养帖是怎么运作的？

所有送养信息均为用户与用户之间的私下约定。FurDaily 不参与任何资金、押金或款项的处理。信息中标注的押金金额仅为发布者单方声明的参考数值，所有付款安排均为双方私下进行，建议在宠物当面交付后再支付。

如何删除我的账号？

前往「设置」页面底部，点击「删除账号和数据」按钮。您的账号将进入 7 天冷静期，期间不重新登录，届时所有数据将被永久清除。

如何屏蔽或举报用户？

点击任意用户头像进入其主页，点击右上角「⋯」菜单即可屏蔽或举报。屏蔽列表可在「设置 → 隐私 → 屏蔽列表」中管理。

我的位置会被公开吗？

不会。FurDaily 只申请「使用期间」的定位权限，且任何对外展示的坐标都会加入 500 米随机偏移。您的精确位置永远不会被分享。

安全与防诈骗

在处理送养或答谢酬金事宜时，请务必保护自己：

  • 始终在公共场所当面交接。
  • 押金或酬金只在宠物实际交付到您手上之后才支付。
  • 通过宠物独有特征（疤痕、体态、对指令的反应等）确认身份。
  • 任何要求预先打款或转账的，极大概率是骗子，请立即举报。
