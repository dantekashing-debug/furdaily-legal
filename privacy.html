Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

FurDaily ("we," "us," or "our") operates the FurDaily mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Phone number (for SMS verification), email address (for email verification), or Apple ID (via Sign in with Apple — Apple user identifier, name, and email as provided by Apple). Display name, bio, and profile avatar.
  • Pet Profiles: Pet name, species, breed, birth date, gender, weight, neutered/spayed status, allergy notes, vaccination records, and pet photos.
  • Health Records: Veterinary visit logs, medication schedules, deworming history, vaccination records, and associated hospital names and costs.
  • Breed Care Calendar: Breed-specific care reminders generated from pet breed, birth date, and general care schedules, including user-customized reminder times.
  • Diary Entries: Text content, photos, mood selections, and quick records (feeding, weight, elimination) related to your pets.
  • Posts & Comments: Text, images, tags, and location labels you choose to share in the community feed.
  • Adoption & Wanted Posts: Pet descriptions, rehoming reasons, adoption requirements, deposit amounts (if stated by the listing user), self-introductions, living environment photos, and budget information.
  • Lost Pet Reports: Pet descriptions, last-seen location, contact phone number, reward details, and sighting submissions.
  • Messages: Text messages, shared images, location shares, and pet cards exchanged with other users.
  • Activity Data: Pet meetup events you create or join, including event title, description, date/time, location (address and coordinates), capacity, activity photos, your registrations (including registered pets), your comments and replies on activities, and reposts of activities.
  • Block & Report Data: The user IDs you block and the content/users you report.
  • Feedback: Any information you voluntarily submit through our feedback form.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, interaction events (likes, comments, follows), and session duration, collected via analytics tools.
  • Device Identifiers: Identifier for Vendor (IDFV) for analytics purposes. We do not collect IDFA.

1.2a Guest Mode (browsing without an account)

If you choose "Guest Mode" on the login screen, you may browse public content without creating an account. In Guest Mode, we do NOT collect any personal identifiers such as phone number, email address, or Apple ID. We continue to collect the Device Information, Usage Data, and Device Identifiers described in Section 1.2 via the third-party SDKs disclosed in Section 3 (Tencent CloudBase for request delivery, Umeng U-App for anonymous usage analytics, Umeng U-APM for crash reports). Location data (Section 1.3) is collected only if you explicitly grant iOS location permission when using location-dependent features (nearby users, nearby services). If you later register or sign in, the server-side user identifier is linked with your account from that point forward; no retroactive linkage is performed on Guest Mode data.

1.3 Information Collected with Your Permission

  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we collect your approximate location (GPS coordinates with ~500-meter randomization applied) to enable nearby user discovery, nearby pet service search, and lost pet map features. You can disable location sharing at any time in your device settings or within the App's privacy toggle.
  • Camera & Photo Library: With your permission, we access your camera to take photos and your photo library to upload or save images.
  • Push Notifications: Push notifications are disabled by default. You must explicitly enable each category (new chat messages, health reminders, nearby lost pets) in Settings → Notifications. The first time you enable a category, iOS will prompt you to grant system-level notification permission. You may revoke permission at any time in iOS Settings or by turning off individual categories in the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and services.
  • Authenticate your identity via SMS verification, email verification, or Sign in with Apple.
  • Display your profile and pet information to other users as you choose.
  • Enable social features including posts, comments, likes, follows, reposts, bookmarks, and messaging.
  • Enable activity/meetup features including event creation, registration, comments, and broadcast notifications to nearby users.
  • Enforce user-initiated blocks by filtering the blocked user's content and notifications in both directions.
  • Show nearby users and pet services based on your location.
  • Facilitate lost pet reporting, sighting submissions, and contact request workflows.
  • Facilitate pet adoption and rehoming through posting and application features.
  • Generate and deliver health care reminders and notifications.
  • Send push notifications for social interactions, health reminders, and system updates.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App experience.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and policy violations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your display name, avatar, bio, posts, comments, activities, and pet profiles are visible to other App users. Your approximate location is only visible if you enable location sharing. Your phone number in lost pet reports is only shared with users you explicitly approve.
  • Service Providers:
    • Tencent CloudBase (Shanghai, China region) — cloud infrastructure for SMS/email/Apple Sign-In token verification, database storage, and file storage.
    • Amap (Gaode Maps) — nearby pet service search (POI lookup). Location queries are sent without account identifiers.
    • Umeng (U-App) Analytics by Alibaba — aggregate usage analytics. Umeng collects: device model, iOS version, App version, Identifier for Vendor (IDFV), session events, and page views. Umeng does NOT collect IDFA, precise GPS coordinates, contacts, or the content of your messages, posts, diary entries, or pet records.
    • Umeng (U-APM) Application Performance Monitoring by Alibaba — crash reports and performance metrics. UMAPM collects: crash stack traces, app launch time, memory/CPU usage snapshots at crash time, device model, iOS version, App version, and network state at the time of the incident. UMAPM does NOT collect IDFA, GPS coordinates, contacts, or the content of your messages, posts, diary entries, or pet records.
    • Apple (Sign in with Apple) — we receive only your Apple user identifier. Name and email are shared only if you choose to at first sign-in; Hide My Email is supported.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on Tencent CloudBase servers in the Shanghai region of mainland China.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS), token-based authentication, server-side ownership validation, and input sanitization.
  • User location coordinates are randomized by approximately 500 meters before being shared with other users.
  • Contact information (phone numbers) in lost pet reports is protected behind an approval-based request system.
  • Despite our efforts, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained for the lifetime of your account plus 7 days after deletion request.
  • Posts, Diary Entries, and Pet Records: Retained until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Notifications: Automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Messages: Retained for the lifetime of your account.
  • Lost Pet Reports: Retained until resolved (pet found) or you delete them.
  • Analytics Data: Retained in aggregated, anonymized form.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and Update: View and edit your profile, pet information, and posts at any time within the App.
  • Delete Your Account: Request account deletion in Settings. Your data will be permanently removed after a 7-day grace period, during which you may cancel the request.
  • Control Location Sharing: Enable or disable location features in your device settings or via the in-app privacy toggle.
  • Manage Notifications: Enable or disable push notifications in your device settings or within the App.
  • Block Users: Block other users from contacting you or viewing your content.
  • Withdraw Consent: You may stop using the App and request data deletion at any time.

For users in the European Economic Area (GDPR), United Kingdom (UK GDPR), or other jurisdictions with similar data protection laws, you may have additional rights including the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act ("CPRA") grants you the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., to complete a transaction, comply with legal obligations, or detect security incidents).
  • Right to Correct: You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. Therefore no opt-out is required, but this right is preserved.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: You may request that we limit the use of your sensitive personal information (precise geolocation) to only purposes necessary for providing the services you requested.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

Categories of Personal Information Collected (last 12 months): identifiers (phone, email, Apple ID, IDFV), customer records (name, avatar), internet/electronic activity (usage, page views), geolocation data (approximate location with ~500m randomization; precise GPS while location feature is active), audio/visual data (photos you upload), and inferences (pet breed care recommendations).

Sensitive Personal Information: We collect precise geolocation only when you explicitly enable location features. It is used solely to power nearby user discovery, nearby service search, and lost pet proximity alerts. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or shared with third parties for their purposes.

How to Submit a Request: Email dantekashing@gmail.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request" and include your registered phone number, email, or Apple ID so we can verify your identity. We will respond within 45 days as required by law. You may also designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it. The App is rated 17+ on the App Store due to user-generated content, direct messaging, and location-based features; users between 13 and 17 years of age should have parental or guardian awareness when using the App.

9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., external map applications). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. International Data Transfers

Important for users outside China: Your information is stored and processed on Tencent CloudBase servers located in the Shanghai region of mainland China. By creating an account and using the App, you expressly consent to the transfer of your personal information to servers in China, where data protection laws may differ from those of your country of residence.

We apply HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit, token-based authentication, and server-side access controls, but you should be aware that Chinese data protection and law enforcement frameworks may differ from U.S. or EU frameworks. If you do not consent to this transfer, you should not use the App.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
